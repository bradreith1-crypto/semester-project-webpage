Part 1: Equipment

Your equipment is very important because faulty equipment can cause physical harm

  • if your clothing isnt warm enough, it can cause sickness such as hypothermia or a common cold
  • if your skis are too long or too short, you wont be able to control your direction correctly
  • if your boots are to big, they could slip off and take your skis with them
